Sojourn-Vacation Planning App

THE PROBLEM: Travelers with families need a way to streamline all of the research involved with planning a trip and still find what they want.

THE SOLUTION: Sojourn is the voice activated trip planner that finds everything you want, within your budget, so you don’t waste time.

Role: UX Designer

Tools: Figma, Miro, Invision

User Research

Research Objectives: Discover what the hardest part of travel planning is and what would make it easier.

Method: Conducted 5 interviews with 18-34 year olds who have at least one child (statistically this is the group that travels the most). Surveyed 10 participants who had taken at least one trip in the past year.

We found that most of our interviewees needed to spend a lot of time planning but disliked how time consuming it was, however they didn’t consider using a travel agent because they wanted to do things themselves.

"I just like to do everything on my own"-Ashley
"We needed a lot more planning, it would have avoided a lot of arguing” -Fern
“I expect it to be a long process...in a way I dread it”. -Tiffany

Survey Data

Our survey revealed that most of our participants traveled with their families and that 80% spent over a day planning vacations. 50% spent anywhere from 4 days to 4 weeks planning!
